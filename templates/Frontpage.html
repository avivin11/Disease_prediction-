Prediction of Diseases . And how we are doing it ...

The symptoms you provide are compared with symptoms of diseases that are stored in our database . We will display diseases that you may be suffering from in order of their probability and a list of doctors available in your city with their details .

Doctor Registeration. And why you should do it ...

By registering with us , we will suggest our users to consult you if they seem to have a disease that you are specialist in . We can help our society in much better way if we work together !

Hospital Registeration. And where we are using it ...

If you register your hospital with us , we will suggest our users to consult doctors from your hospital . For that we strongly reccomend you that you should register all your doctors wiyh us .
